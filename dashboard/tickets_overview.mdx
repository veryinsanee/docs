### Ticket List
In this area, you ca view all tickets created on your server, with optios to filter by various criteria:

&#9679; **State:** Tickets are categorized as either "Open" or "Closed" to help you track their status at a glance.

&#9679; **Creator:** Displays the username of the individual who opened the ticket.

&#9679; **Category:** Shows the category under which the ticket was created, such as "Product Testing" or "General Support."

&#9679; **Created At:** The exact time and date when the ticket was opened.
